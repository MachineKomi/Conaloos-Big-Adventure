# Mommy

**Sprite:** `peep_mommy_F_30ish.png`
**Voice:** Bright, observant, quick to wonder. Treats every fact like a little gift she gets to hand over.
**Catchphrase:** *"Oh -- look at THAT."*
**Obsession:** Noticing. The shadows of leaves on a wall. The exact shade of evening. The way Amelia hums when she draws.

## Backstory

The mum. In the world of the game she shows up as the eyes of the world — pointing out things the child might have missed, but never directing.

## Sample dialogue (draft)

1. *"Oh look at that light, how it lands on the floor. / It paints a small map to the back of the door."*
2. *"The leaves of this tree are a hundred small hands, / Each waving hello to the wind where it stands."*
3. *"A song in your head is a song that's *for* you. / It came in this morning with nothing to do."*
4. *"I love when you wonder. I love when you ask. / I love that your questions don't fit in a flask."*
5. *"There's tea in the pot. There is bread in the bowl. / There's room in the day for a slow little stroll."*

## Where they appear

- Indoor scenes; garden scenes; nature scenes where a "look closer" moment is needed.

## Lessons they tend to drop

- **science** — noticing-as-science, observation, light, weather.
- **art-history** — colour, composition, the world as a painting.
- **emotions** — gentle naming, modelling curiosity.

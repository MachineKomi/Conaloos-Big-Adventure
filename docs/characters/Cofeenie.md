# Cofeenie, Queen of Rabbits (Twin)

**Sprite:** `animal_Cofeenie_Queen-of-Rabbits-Twin.png`
**Voice:** Royal, but in the way a four-year-old does royal: dignified, slightly bossy, very kind underneath.
**Catchphrase:** *"By order of me, the queen --"*
**Obsession:** Decrees. Tiny ones. ("All the ferns shall be friendlier today." "No one is to be sad before lunch.")
**Twin of:** Lucy (see `Lucy.md`).

## Backstory

One half of the Queens of Rabbits — there are two queens, both rabbits, both somehow ruling at the same time and somehow agreeing on everything that matters. The arrangement is very modern.

## Sample dialogue (draft)

1. *"By order of me, the queen Cofeenie -- / Today is for hopping. (And nibbling weenies.)"*
2. *"My sister and I share a kingdom of green. / She's also the queen. So, you see, two are seen."*
3. *"A queen of the rabbits is not very tall. / But her ears are her crown, and her crown hears it all."*
4. *"To rule is to listen, then think, then declare: / 'I have heard you, my friend. I shall pass you a pear.'"*
5. *"The grass has decided. The grass has agreed. / Today I shall offer you carrots, indeed."*

## Where they appear

- Outdoor green scenes — fields, gardens, meadows. Always with Lucy if possible.

## Lessons they tend to drop

- **animals** — rabbit ears, hopping, what rabbits eat, why their noses twitch.
- **culture-history** — what a queen does (gentle, true: a leader, a listener), the idea of co-rulers.
- **emotions** — kindness as a rule, not a feeling.

## Possible remixes

- Cofeenie + Lucy together: "Two queens are a rare and a wonderful thing, / They share their decrees -- and they share every spring."
- Cofeenie wearing the rocketship as a crown: silly, rare-response only.

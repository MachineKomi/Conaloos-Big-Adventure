# Amelia the Lead Designer

**Sprite:** `peep_Amelia_F_4.png`
**Voice:** Sentences that veer mid-thought, then come back surprised by where they've been. She's four; the world is large and recently arrived.
**Catchphrase:** *"...and then what?"*
**Obsession:** Naming things. Every cloud, every dog, every speck of dust deserves a name.

## Backstory

The lead creative on this whole adventure. The drawings are hers. The character names are hers. If a line in the game sounds odd, it's because she said it that way and she was right.

## Sample dialogue (draft)

1. *"I'm Amelia, and Amelia is me, / And I name all the things, every-one that I see."*
2. *"That cloud is called Pinky. That stone there is Sue. / That puddle is Wandle. (And so, now, are you.)"*
3. *"My favourite of colours? Today it is blue. / Tomorrow's a different. I'll tell you it too."*
4. *"A question's a thing that just falls out your mouth. / It points to the north, but it walks to the south."*
5. *"I'm small, but the small can decide really lots: / Where bunnies go visit. What lives in the pots."*

## Where they appear

- Anywhere. She is the protagonist; place her in the hub at minimum.

## Lessons they tend to drop

- **language** — naming, words as a kind of magic.
- **emotions** — naming feelings is also naming things.
- **philosophy** — questions matter; you don't always need answers.

## Possible remixes

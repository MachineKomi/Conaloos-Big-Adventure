# Seesa, the Pink Bee

**Sprite:** `animal_Seesa_pink-bee.png`
**Voice:** Bright, fast, slightly out of breath. Seesa speaks the way a bee flies — small bursts, then a hover. Her sentences sometimes end before they end.
**Catchphrase:** *"-- and -- and -- and!"*
**Obsession:** Flowers, and which flower is BEST today. Her opinion on this changes every six minutes.

## Backstory

A pink bee. The "pink" matters: she is, in fact, pink, which she will tell you. She is one of those bees who decided, on principle, that yellow-and-black was not for her. She's still a bee. She just made a choice.

## Sample dialogue (draft)

1. *"I'm Seesa! And Seesa is pink, which is rare -- / There aren't many pink bees, but there's one of me. (There.)"*
2. *"The daisies are SPECTACULAR! All of them! Each! / -- well, except for the wilted. -- but those are a teach!"*
3. *"I buzz, and I buzz, and I land, and I sip, / And I tell every flower that flowers are HIP."*
4. *"A bee is a hum that has stuck to a wing, / And the wing is a thing that decided to sing."*
5. *"I saw the queens twice today, hopping a-by. / They nodded. I nodded. (My nod is a fly.)"*

## Where they appear

- **sunny-rocket-garden** — most natural place; she's a garden bee.
- Wherever there are flowers. Could appear briefly in the cottage if a vase is shown.

## Lessons they tend to drop

- **animals** — bees, pollination (gently), the idea that creatures choose where they go.
- **language** — onomatopoeia (buzz, hum), invented affectionate insults ("hip!").
- **emotions** — enthusiasm as a way of being; it's okay to love things loudly.

## Possible remixes

- Seesa as a tiny passenger on the rocketship's nose: rare-response, *"I'm an astronaut bee! Just a small one! For now! / I shall pollinate planets! ...I don't know quite how!"*

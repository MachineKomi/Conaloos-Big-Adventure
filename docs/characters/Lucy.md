# Lucy, Queen of Rabbits (Twin)

**Sprite:** `animal_Lucy_Queen-of-Rabbits-Twin.png`
**Voice:** Quieter than Cofeenie. Speaks in observations rather than decrees. Where Cofeenie issues an order, Lucy notices a thing.
**Catchphrase:** *"...did you see that?"*
**Obsession:** Tiny things. Ants. Dewdrops. The rim of a leaf.
**Twin of:** Cofeenie (see `Cofeenie.md`).

## Backstory

Co-queen with Cofeenie. They are very different and rule the same kingdom and never disagree on anything important. Lucy is the watcher; Cofeenie is the speaker. Together they are complete.

## Sample dialogue (draft)

1. *"Did you see that small ant? She is busy. She's small. / But the world that she carries is bigger than tall."*
2. *"My sister, Cofeenie, will tell you what's done. / I'll tell you what's lovely. Together, we're one."*
3. *"A dew on a daisy is just like a star. / Except dew is closer, and star is more far."*
4. *"To watch is to love a small thing for a while -- / Until the thing wanders, and you keep its smile."*
5. *"I'm queen of the very small. Cofeenie's the rest. / Between us, the kingdom is mostly impressed."*

## Where they appear

- Outdoor scenes — especially anywhere with small creatures. Pairs with Cofeenie.

## Lessons they tend to drop

- **animals** — small creatures, behaviours of insects and small mammals.
- **science** — observation as a method.
- **emotions** — care as attention.

## Possible remixes

# Daddy

**Sprite:** `peep_daddy_M_30ish.png`
**Voice:** Calm, slightly tired, secretly delighted by everything Amelia says. Responds in short rhymes that feel like overheard humming.
**Catchphrase:** *"Mm, I think you're right about that."*
**Obsession:** Engineering small kindnesses — making sure the toast is the right amount of brown, the bedtime story has the right voice, the puddle has been jumped in.

## Backstory

The dad. Co-conspirator on this game. In the world of the game, he's a quiet warm presence who shows up when needed and otherwise lets the children lead.

## Sample dialogue (draft)

1. *"A cup of warm something, and someone to share. / The two of those, mostly, is most of my care."*
2. *"You asked me a question, and now I am thinking. / It might take a minute. (Don't worry — I'm blinking.)"*
3. *"I love you in mornings, in middles, and ends. / I love you in colours. I love you in friends."*
4. *"That puddle is round and it's deep and it's blue. / I'd ask you to mind it -- but you won't. So: through!"*
5. *"The clock ticks at me. I tick back at the clock. / We have, at this point, an arrangement. We talk."*

## Where they appear

- Indoor scenes; bedtime / lullaby scenes; anywhere needing warmth.

## Lessons they tend to drop

- **emotions** — naming and accepting feelings, modelling steadiness.
- **economics** (gentle) — sharing, "enough."
- **culture-history** — the small rituals of a household.

## Possible remixes

- Daddy holding / next to mommy in tucked-in scenes — a quiet still frame, no rhyme.

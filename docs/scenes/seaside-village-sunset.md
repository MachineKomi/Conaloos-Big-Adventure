## Educational notes

- **Art history:** sunsets are painters' favourite light — why? Because the sun is filtering through more atmosphere at low angle, which scatters out the blue and leaves orange/red. (We say this gently and without jargon.)
- **Science:** waves come in sets; the moon pulls them; the sound never quite repeats.
- **Language:** seagull cries — onomatopoeia and the idea that we *give* sounds names.

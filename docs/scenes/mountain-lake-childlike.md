## Educational notes

- **Science:** a lake is rain that has chosen to stay. Water reflects light back at us. Mountains are where the earth has wrinkled up over a long time.
- **Animals:** Lucy points out the small lives by the water — ants, beetles, water-skaters.
- **Philosophy:** the reflection question — when you look in a lake, is the lake looking back?
